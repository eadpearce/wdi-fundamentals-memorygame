Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku「神経衰弱」, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face-down on a surface and two cards are flipped face-up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
